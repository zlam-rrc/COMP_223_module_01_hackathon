Essential for team composition and strategy, each role has specific responsibilities and plays a different part in achieving victory. Here's a breakdown of the primary roles you might encounter:

Bot Laner

Often filled by heroes that have strong basic attack whom start out weak in the early game but starts to scale into becoming high value for the team late game by dishing out highly sustained damage.

  • Main responsibilities: Stay safe by remaining close to Support role and behind everyone else in order to deal sustained damage to the enemy heroes.
  • Goals: Focus on farming for items to help supplement damage, be alert of proper positing when engaging in team fights
  • Also known as: AD Carry/ADC/Carry/DPS

Support

Though the name is self-explanatory, it's more than just that. They help the team with other things such as providing map vision, engaging in crowd control, healing or protecting teammates, amongst various other utilities provided in their toolkit.

  • Main responsibilities: Set up plays, assist in securing objects, and provide consistent support for your teammates
  • Goals: Focus on assisting Bot Laner as you are considered a duo in the early stages of the game, then provide more utility to the team later on
  • Also known as: Sup
  • Example characters:

Mid Laner

Often as equivalent in high damage output as the Bottom Laner, but through abilities provided in their toolkit rather than standard basic attacks.

  • Main responsibilities: Use abilities to make significant plays or control the map
  • Goals: Leverage your position in one of the safest parts of the map by bullying the opposing Mid Laner, using your advantage in a centralized position to gain map control and presence throughout
  • Also known as: Mid, Mage, Assassin, AP Carry
  • Example characters:

Top Laner

The main front line against the enemy team, serving as the tank by absorbing most of the opposing team's damage and allowing your own team to retaliate with some of their own. Otherwise, pressuring their own lane by split-pushing.

  • Goals: Stay alive for as long as possible when engaged in team fights so that your Bottom Laner and Mid Laner can both output high opposing damage safely behind you.
