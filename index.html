Introduction to MOBA

This page aims to introduce you to the wonderful world of MOBAs

Beginning life as a mod for a game called StarCraft back in 2002, the MOBA genre has taken the world by storm.

Boasting deep customization and high skill ceiling gameplay, MOBAs offer hundreds or thosuands of hours of riveting experiences.

But what exactly is a MOBA? On this page, you will learn all about the mobile online battle arena genre.

MOBA as a definition is slightly nebulous; there are many MOBAs and each has a unique take on the genre.

But there are some key features a MOBA requires to be a MOBA.

The first are characters. Each boasting a unique set of abilities, your character defines your playstyle and role within the game.

Selecting a tough warrior might mean you are supposed to lead the charge, while an unarmored mage should support from afar.

The second aspect of MOBAs are bases, and the defences they contain.

Primarily, your objective is to attack the enemy base where they spawn from.

The game ends when you destroy their base, or they destroy yours.

Along the way, you will encounter some form of a damaging obstacle you must defeat with the help of NPC minions.

Defeating the enemy minions and obstacles rewards resources, which can be spend improving your character for that match.
